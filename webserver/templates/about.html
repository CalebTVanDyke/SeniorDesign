About

The SIDS alert device allows you to keep track of important vitals of your infant while they are sleeping. The device will monitor your infants heart rate, blood oxygen saturation, and body temperature. You can view the live data that is being read by the device, and you cal also view past data that has been saved. If the device notices and abnormal vitals it will sent an alert to an receivers you would like. It can send both email and text message. If you are not receiving emails please check your spam folder for these emails.
